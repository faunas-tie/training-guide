Two new machines — Shoulder Press (OSSP) and Bicep Curl (OSBC). OSSP is counter-balanced; start at 7 kg and climb to a real working weight. On OSCP and the row, reps before pins.
Warm-Up — ~8 min

Wake the shoulders and upper back before you press, row and curl. Then do each machine's own light set.

01
Shoulder rolls + arm circles — 30 s. Raise the temperature, loosen the joint.
02
Band pull-aparts — 2 × 15. Squeeze the shoulder blades. No band? 12 slow prone 'T' raises.
03
Towel pass-throughs — 8–10. Wide grip overhead and back, arms straight. Opens the shoulders for pressing.
04
Scapular wall slides — 8–10. Goalpost arms, slide up and down keeping hands and elbows on the wall.
05
Dead bug (unloaded) — 5 per side. Primes the trunk for the plank.
Exercises
01 / 05
Machine Chest Press
Life Fitness Optima · OSCP · 3 × 10–12 @ 14 kg · Rest 90 sec
Seated press for chest, front shoulders and triceps, path guided by the machine.
Quick reset
  • Back flush; handles at mid-chest; elbows ~45°.
Tap when done
Key cues
  • Blades down and back — don't shrug toward the ears.
  • Elbows at 45° — tucked is stronger and easier on the shoulder.
  • Soft lockout, 2 s back.
Reps before pins: aim for the top of the rep range at 14 kg with clean form before adding a pin. Log reps + RPE.
02 / 05
Single-Arm Cable Row
Dual Adjustable Pulley · OSDAP · 3 × 10/side @ 7.5 kg · Rest 60–90 sec
Standing one-arm row on the cable column. Working one side at a time forces the trunk to resist twisting — the same anti-rotation bracing you do every stride.
Quick reset
  • Pulley at lower-rib height; taut cable; soft knees, long flat back.
Tap when done
Key cues
  • Lead with the elbow — the hand just holds on.
  • Don't twist — resisting rotation is the exercise.
  • Full reach forward, 2 s out.
Work at 7.5 kg and only add 2.5 kg once you complete all sets per side with ~2 reps to spare. Log the working weight each arm + any L/R gap.

03 / 05
Machine Shoulder Press
Life Fitness Optima · OSSP · 3 × 10 @ 7 kg — calibrate · Rest 90 sec
Seated overhead press — shoulders and triceps — path guided by the machine. The work arm is counter-balanced, so the lowest pins feel light; climb to find a real working weight.
Machine setup
Head, upper back and lower back against the pad, feet flat.
Seat-height lever so the handles start at about shoulder height.
Straight wrists, knuckles pointing up where you'll press; elbows under/just in front of the wrists.
Start the pin light to learn the path, then add load to calibrate.
Tap when done
Instructions
  1. Brace — ribs down, back flat, feet planted.
  2. Press smoothly overhead to a soft lockout — don't snap straight.
  3. Lower under control (2 s) back to shoulder height.
Key cues
  • Ribs down — press from the shoulders, don't arch the lower back.
  • No shrug — keep the neck long.
  • Soft lockout — stop just short of straight.
Counter-balanced, so 7 kg — calibrate is just a starting point. Run set 1 there; if it's easy, climb in 7 kg steps until a set leaves ~2 reps in reserve. Log whatever pin that turns out to be.
▶ First time on this machine
04 / 05
Machine Bicep Curl
Life Fitness Optima · OSBC · 2 × 12 @ 7 kg — calibrate · Rest 60–90 sec
Seated curl with the upper arms on a pad — isolates the biceps and brachialis. Counter-balanced and a smaller stack (tops out at 63 kg).
Machine setup
Seat height so the backs of the upper arms rest flat on the pad, armpits near its top edge.
Self-aligning handles; start with arms nearly straight, a soft elbow bend.
Pin light first to learn the arc, then calibrate up.
Tap when done
Instructions
  1. Curl with the arms staying on the pad — don't lift the elbows to cheat.
  2. Full range; squeeze at the top, 2 s down to a near-straight arm.
Key cues
  • Arms stay on the pad.
  • Full range — squeeze top, control the bottom.
  • 2 s down — the lowering builds the muscle.
Counter-balanced — work where 12 reps leaves ~2 in reserve. Log the pin.
05 / 05
Front Plank
Bodyweight · 3 × 45 s BW · Rest 60 sec
Anti-extension core hold — the trunk braces rigid against gravity, the same job it does mid-stride.
Quick reset
  • Forearms under shoulders; straight line head to heels.
Tap when done
Key cues
  • Straight line — no sag, no pike.
  • Squeeze glutes + brace abs.
  • Quality over the clock — stop when the hips sag.
Cool-Down — ~6–8 min

Targets what you just worked — chest, shoulders, upper back, arms — plus today's daily hip-flexor stretch.

01
Doorway / pec stretch — 45 s per side. Opens the pecs after pressing.
02
Cross-body shoulder + overhead triceps — 30 s and 20 s per side.
03
Lat stretch — 30 s per side. Eases the upper back after the rows.
04
Thoracic extension / child's pose with reach — 45 s. Decompress after the bracing.
05
Half-kneeling hip-flexor stretch — 45–90 s per side. Today's daily hip-flexor work — ticked off.
